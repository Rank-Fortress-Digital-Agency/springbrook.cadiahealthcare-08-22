What is Cadia Healthcare Springbrook and what do they offer


Cadia Healthcare Springbrook is a comprehensive rehabilitation program that offers on-site renal dialysis, nursing care, dietary services, and care transitions. The goal of the program is to help patients regain their independence and improve their quality of life. The program offers a variety of services to meet the needs of each individual patient.

The on-site renal dialysis program provides patients with the necessary treatments to improve kidney function. The nursing staff provides patients with around-the-clock care and support. The dietary team works with patients to develop a healthy eating plan that meets their individual needs. The care transition team assists patients in making a smooth transition from the hospital to home or another care facility.

Each patient’s care plan is individualized to meet their specific needs. The care team at Cadia Healthcare Springbrook is committed to providing the highest quality of care to each and every patient.
